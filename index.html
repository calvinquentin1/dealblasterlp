Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a real estate license to use Deal Blaster?

No. Deal Blaster generates creative finance offer terms for you to present to agents. You do not need a license to wholesale real estate in most states, but always check your local laws.

What is Propwire and do I have to use it?

Propwire is a free property data platform where you can export lists of MLS properties filtered by days on market, price, and location. You can use any data source that exports a CSV file — Propwire is just the one we recommend because it is free.

Does this work in my market?

Yes. Deal Blaster works with any MLS market in the United States. As long as there are properties sitting on the MLS for 90 days or more in your area, you have motivated sellers to send offers to.

What kind of offers does Deal Blaster generate?

Deal Blaster generates seller finance, subject-to, and hybrid creative finance offer terms based on the property data in your CSV. All offers are calculated automatically — no manual math required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Deal Blaster is $200 a month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time from your account dashboard.
